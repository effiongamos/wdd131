Site Plan for Estamabenco Baking/Catering

Site Name

Estamabenco Baking/Catering

This name was selected because it clearly represents the family business's focus on baking and catering,
particularly specializing in Puff-Puff, a popular West African snack.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide information about Estamabenco Baking/Catering's offerings,
including the history of the business, the types of products available, and the ability to order products online.
It will also serve as a platform to promote the cultural significance of Puff-Puff.

Scenarios

  • How can I place an order for Puff-Puff online?
  • What can i do to make our customers feel welcome and see the important of west african snack Puff-Puff?

Color Schema

Main Color: #b5651d (Brown) - used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: #5f5f5f (Gray) - used for background highlights.

Text Color: #000000 (Black) - used for paragraphs.

Accent 1 Color: #FFA500 (Orange) - used for buttons, links, and call-to-action elements.

Accent 2 Color: #fff (White) - used for background areas and subtle highlights.

Accent 3 Color: #FF6347 (Tomato Red) - used for highlights and decorative elements.

Accent 4 Color: #3CB371 (Medium Sea Green) - used for additional accents and visual interest.

Typography

Main Font: Roboto, sans-serif - used for body text

Accent Font: Lora, serif - used for special emphasis in paragraphs or quotes and Headings.
